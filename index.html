<p class="mt-3">JWoC ( JGEC Winter of Code) is a program organised by

<!-- <br> -->
<div class="col-lg-12 col-12 text-white">
<h2 class="section-heading" style="color: white;
text-shadow: 2px 2px 4px blue;
font-weight: 500;
margin-top: 10px;">JWoC-HOW
IT WORKS?</h2>
<p class="mt-3">Participants (restrained to 1st and 2nd year undergraduate students)
participate for the
program. Mentors willing to Open Source their projects in JWoC submit their proposals. The
best projects
are selected for this program. Students get acquainted about the projects from the mentors
during the
Community Bonding Period. Students work on these projects during the coding phase. At the
end of the
coding period, the winners of the programs are announced on the basis of their contribution
in terms of
quantity as well as quality.<br>
For more information on the event download the student or mentor manual respectively.</p>
